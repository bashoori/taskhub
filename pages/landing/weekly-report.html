Step 4

Your weekly report

This is the core of the service. The report is designed to be read in minutes, understood instantly, and used to decide what to do next.

What this report is (and is not)

  • ✔ A clear summary of what happened this week
  • ✔ Focused on trends and changes, not raw numbers
  • ✔ Written for humans, not analysts
  • ✘ Not a complex dashboard
  • ✘ Not dozens of charts with no explanation

What’s inside the report

1. Weekly snapshot

A quick overview of key numbers and how they changed compared to last week.

  • Total leads / records
  • Increase or decrease vs previous week
  • Immediate red flags or wins

2. Performance breakdown

We break results down by what matters most to you.

  • Source, campaign, location, or category
  • Best and worst performers
  • Clear comparisons

3. Trends & insights

This is where numbers turn into understanding.

  • What’s improving?
  • What’s declining?
  • What changed and why it likely happened

4. Next actions

Every report ends with concrete recommendations.

  • What to continue doing
  • What to stop or fix
  • What to test next week

Delivery format

  • PDF report (easy to read and share)
  • Clean Excel / CSV file (organized and reusable)
You can forward the PDF to a manager or client without explaining anything. The report explains itself.

What this gives you every week

  • Clarity instead of guessing
  • Confidence in decisions
  • A repeatable weekly rhythm
