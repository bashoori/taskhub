Step 4

Your weekly report

This is the core of the service. The report is designed to be read in minutes, understood instantly, and used to decide what to do next.

What this report is (and is not)

  • ✔ A clear summary of what happened this week
  • ✔ Focused on trends and changes
  • ✔ Written for humans
  • ✘ Not a complex dashboard
  • ✘ Not charts without explanation

What’s inside the report

1. Weekly snapshot

  • Total leads / records
  • Week-over-week change
  • Immediate red flags or wins

2. Performance breakdown

  • Source, campaign, or category
  • Best and worst performers
  • Clear comparisons

3. Trends & insights

  • What’s improving
  • What’s declining
  • Why it likely happened

4. Next actions

  • What to continue
  • What to fix
  • What to test next week

Delivery format

  • PDF report
  • Clean Excel / CSV file
The report explains itself. No walkthrough required.

What this gives you every week

  • Clarity
  • Confidence
  • A repeatable weekly rhythm
