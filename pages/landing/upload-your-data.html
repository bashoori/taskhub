Step 1

Upload your data

This step is intentionally simple. You don’t need to prepare anything perfectly. Just send what you already have.

What you can send

  • Excel files (.xlsx)
  • CSV files (.csv)
  • Google Sheets
  • CRM exports (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
If you’re not sure which file is “right”, send all of them. We’ll figure it out.

What’s okay (and expected)

  • Messy columns
  • Duplicate rows
  • Missing values
  • Inconsistent formats

Cleaning data is our job. You are not expected to fix anything before sending it.

What we do after you upload

  • Review the structure of your data
  • Identify issues and gaps
  • Prepare it for cleaning and reporting
