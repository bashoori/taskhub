How it works

A simple system that turns raw lead data into a clear, easy-to-read weekly report — without dashboards, setup, or long-term commitment.

From leads to reports in three steps

1. Upload your leads

Upload one CSV file with up to 50 leads. The file can come from a contact form, spreadsheet, CRM export, or any single lead source you already use.

2. We clean and summarize

During the beta, submissions are reviewed manually. We clean and organize your leads into a consistent structure so the information is easy to understand.

Your data is never tracked, reused, or sold. It is used only to generate your report.

3. You receive a weekly report

You receive a simple weekly snapshot by email. It shows total leads, new leads, and changes compared to the previous week — all in one view.

What you get

A calm, readable report that gives you clarity on what’s happening with your leads at a glance.

No dashboards. No integrations. No configuration.
