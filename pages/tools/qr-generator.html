What Is a QR Code?

A QR code (Quick Response Code) is a type of barcode that can store information such as URLs, text, contact data, WiFi credentials, or business details. By scanning the code with a smartphone camera, users can instantly access the stored information.

Why Use QR Codes?

QR codes are widely used in marketing, business automation, product packaging, events, and more because they:

  • Make information accessible with a single scan
  • Improve engagement on printed materials
  • Save space on business cards or posters
  • Provide a seamless experience for mobile users

Common Uses of QR Codes

  • Website links
  • Social media profiles
  • Digital menus
  • Event check-in pages
  • WiFi login information
  • Business contact cards (vCard)
