About Bita Digital Hub

Bita Digital Hub is a personal tech studio created and run by Bita Ashoori, a data engineer based in Vancouver, Canada. The focus is on building practical digital tools, data solutions, and clear educational resources around data, cloud, and automation.

What Bita Digital Hub Is

Personal Tech Studio

This is not an agency or a marketplace. It is a hands-on workspace where real-world data engineering, automation, and tooling are designed, tested, and shared.

Practical Engineering

The work here focuses on real implementations: data pipelines, SQL, Python workflows, cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and GCP, and lightweight automation systems.

Clear Learning Resources

Educational content is example-driven and practical, designed to reduce confusion and help people understand how systems actually work.

Who It’s For

Learners & Beginners

People learning data engineering, cloud, or automation who want clear explanations instead of buzzwords.

Professionals

Engineers and analysts who want practical tools, reference implementations, and simplified workflows.

Small Teams & Individuals

People who need useful digital tools and automations without complex platforms or heavy setup.

About the Founder

Bita Ashoori

Bita Ashoori is a data engineer with experience in SQL Server, ETL pipelines, Python-based workflows, cloud platforms, and automation. A background in teaching and mentoring strongly influences how tools and content are designed: structured, clear, and practical.

Location
