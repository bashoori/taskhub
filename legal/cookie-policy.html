This Cookie Policy explains how Bita Digital Hub uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Website analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising personalization (Google AdSense)
  • Security and performance optimization (Cloudflare)
  • Saving simple preferences such as tool settings

3. Types of Cookies

  • Essential cookies – required for basic website operation
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how users interact with the website
  • Advertising cookies – used by third-party ad networks for recommendations

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties to set cookies on this website, including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google AdSense
  • Cloudflare

5. Cookie Control

  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google ad settings
