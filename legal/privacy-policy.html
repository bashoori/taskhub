Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Bita Digital Hub collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Last updated: December 2025

1. Who We Are

Bita Digital Hub is a personal tech studio operated by Bita Ashoori, a data engineer based in Vancouver, Canada. The site provides digital tools, educational content, and technical resources related to data, cloud technologies, automation, and reporting.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the site.

Information you provide directly

  • Email address when you contact us
  • Any information you choose to include in your message

Information collected automatically

  • Pages visited, browser type, and device type
  • This information does not personally identify you

3. How Information Is Used

  • Respond to messages and inquiries
  • Improve site functionality and content
  • Understand site usage at an aggregate level

4. Cookies and Analytics

Cookies or similar technologies may be used to analyze traffic and improve site performance. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Trusted third-party providers (such as hosting or analytics services) may collect limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Data may be disclosed only when required by law.

7. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information, but no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. External Links

This website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

9. Children’s Information

Bita Digital Hub does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
