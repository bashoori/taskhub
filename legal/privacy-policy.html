Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Bita Digital Hub collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Last updated: December 2025

1. Who We Are

Bita Digital Hub is a personal tech studio operated by Bita Ashoori, a data engineer based in Vancouver, Canada. The website provides digital tools, educational content, and technical resources related to data, cloud technologies, and automation.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the site and improve its content.

Information you may provide directly

  • Email address, if you contact us by email or through a contact form
  • Any information you choose to include in your message

Information collected automatically

  • Basic usage data such as pages visited, browser type, and device type
  • This data does not personally identify you

3. How Information Is Used

  • Respond to messages or inquiries
  • Improve site functionality and content
  • Understand site usage at an aggregate level

4. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to understand traffic patterns and improve usability. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Trusted third-party services (hosting, analytics) may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data except when legally required.

7. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data, but no internet transmission is completely secure.

8. External Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

9. Children’s Information

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Policy Updates

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
